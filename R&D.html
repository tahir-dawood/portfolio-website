Involvement in   R&D

Involvement in Research and Development (R&D) activities that bridge academia and industry is crucial for fostering innovation, advancing technology, and driving economic growth. Collaboration encourages the training and development of the next generation of scientists, engineers, and researchers. Students gain hands-on experience and exposure to industry practices. Here's a summary of my involvement as voluntary general secretary of IMechE Pakistan group in academia under the flagship event of IMechE.

Abul Kalam Design Challenge

AKDC, based on the Design Challenge being run by IMeche UK, focuses on giving the freshman engineering students an opportunity to experience a complete product development cycle from the initial design, to the fabrication, testing and product presentation. The students design a solution for a real-life engineering problem while staying within the constraints given. The Challenge is run every year with the guidance and advice of Dr. David Ball, Founder Design Challenge UK, who also attended the Finale in person in 2018 in pakistan.

Speak Out for Engineering (SOfE) Presentation Competition

SOfE Pakistan is the part of the global network of SOfE Competitions being organized by IMechE UK, for YM of the Institution. The competitions seeks to challenge and groom the verbal and visual communication skills of the participants in explaining Mechanical Engineering based concepts. SOfE Pakistan starts of with a Local Heat in each student chapter and Industrial Heats.
